About Tokyo Homme

Tokyo Homme is a new twist on luxurious living in Tokyo. Energized by visual excitement and a single point of view celebrating craftsmanship, insight and humour, this is a series of storeys and artefacts carefully assembled in the world's largest metropolis.

Tokyo Homme was founded in 2018 as a luxury compendium of men's style, grooming & lifestyle in Tokyo. Originally focused on the international business visitor, who needed a no nonsense guide to the city. From where to get a good shirt, to where to take colleagues for dinner.

Japan’s No. 1 Men's luxury English Magazine.

With a emphasis on future activities, exhibits and news — including the city's most extensive listing of activities, news from society, and sartorial guides — comprehensively guides to what's happening in the world's largest capital city.

The best bars and restaurants are monitored by our flagship website and varied social media channels. Keeping the cream of the Tokyo community linked, updated and energised.

Tokyo Homme's audience of linked, wealthy & influntial Tokyoites continues to expand. At our heart, however, is the simple conviction that there will always be a place for a print brand that is dedicated to telling fresh storeys, sending photographers on assignments, and ensuring that the readers, advertisers and partners who have helped us along the way have all their success.
